Tyler Hynes

Canadian Actor

About

Tyler Hynes is a Canadian actor, director, producer, editor, and writer, best known for his work in Hallmark Channel films.
Born on May 6, 1986, in Toronto, Ontario, and raised in Ottawa, he began his professional acting career at the age of eight,
performing in a 72-performance run of "A Christmas Carol" and later touring Canada in "The Who's Tommy" as young Tommy .​
Hynes has built a diverse acting portfolio, including roles in television series such as "Saving Hope"
(as Luke Reid), "19-2" (as Vince Legare), and "UnREAL" (as Luke). However, he is most recognized for his leading roles in Hallmark Channel
movies, where he has become a fan favorite.​
Some of his notable Hallmark films include:
"It Was Always You" (2021) – A romantic drama where he stars alongside Erin Krakow IMDb.
"Three Wise Men and a Baby" (2022) – A holiday comedy featuring Hynes and two other Hallmark leading men.​
"Holiday Touchdown: A Chiefs Love Story" (2024) – A unique collaboration between Hallmark and the NFL, where Hynes plays Derrick, the Kansas City Chiefs' Director of Fan Engagement .​
"Shifting Gears" (2024) – A romantic film co-starring Katherine Barrell .​
In addition to acting, Hynes has directed and produced several projects, showcasing his versatility in the entertainment industry.​
